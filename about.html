About CipherX

CipherX is a community for hackers, coders, and creators who dare to push boundaries. We provide a platform to learn, collaborate, and build projects in coding, cybersecurity, AI, and more.

From beginners exploring their first lines of code to advanced enthusiasts diving into ethical hacking and cryptography, CipherX welcomes all curious minds. Together, we break the code and build the future.

Members get opportunities to contribute to open-source, host workshops, participate in hackathons, and showcase their work to the wider tech community.

Venue

Anu Infotech is the ideal venue for hackers, coders, and creators to collaborate, learn, and innovate. Equipped for workshops, coding sessions, and meetups, it offers a modern and inspiring environment for all tech enthusiasts.
